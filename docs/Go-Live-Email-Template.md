**Subject:** Merlin goes live [GO-LIVE DATE] — hands-free warranty stories for our service team

Team,

Starting **[GO-LIVE DATE]**, we are launching **Merlin** — our new Mercedes-Benz warranty story tool. Merlin helps you turn your repair notes into professional, submission-ready warranty narratives in minutes instead of typing everything at the end of the job.

### What is Merlin?

Merlin is a secure tablet app built for the shop floor. You document what you actually found and fixed. Merlin formats that into a clear warranty story you can paste into CDK or export as a PDF.

**The biggest daily benefit: hands-free voice input.** Talk your technician notes while you work — Merlin listens, even in a noisy bay. No more hunting for a keyboard with greasy gloves.

### What Merlin does — and does not do

| Merlin does | Merlin does not |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Turn your documented notes into a polished story | Invent test results you did not perform |
| Let you speak notes with tap-to-toggle or push-to-talk | Replace your judgment on warranty claims |
| Help you review, edit, and copy stories for CDK | Work without Wi‑Fi for AI generation (typing always works) |

### Voice input — quick preview

- **Tap to toggle (default):** Tap the mic once to start, tap again to stop — great for longer notes.
- **Push-to-talk:** Tap the hand icon, then **hold** the mic while you speak — best near running lifts and air tools.

Manual typing always works on every field. Voice is a shortcut, not a requirement.
